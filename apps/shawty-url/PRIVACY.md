# Privacy Policy for Shawty URL

**Last updated: June 22, 2026**

Shawty URL ("the app") is a free Android app that shortens links shared from other apps. This policy explains what data the app handles and, just as importantly, what it does not.

## Summary

Shawty URL does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its developer. It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or user accounts. The only network communication the app performs is sending the URL you choose to shorten to a third-party shortening service you implicitly select by using the app.

## What data is processed

**The link you share.** When you share a link to Shawty URL from another app (e.g. your browser or Google Maps), the app extracts the URL from the shared text and sends it to a link-shortening service — [is.gd](https://is.gd) by default, falling back to [v.gd](https://v.gd) if is.gd is unavailable (this fallback can be turned off in Settings). This is the only data sent off your device, and it is sent only to that shortening service, not to the developer of Shawty URL. is.gd and v.gd are operated by a third party, not by Shawty URL's developer; see their own privacy/usage policies for how they handle that request.

**Optional local history.** If you turn on "Keep history" in Settings, the app stores a record of each original URL, its shortened version, and a timestamp — entirely on your device, using a local database. This history is never transmitted anywhere. It is only used to show you your past links inside the app, and you can delete individual entries or clear it all from the History screen at any time. History is off by default.

**App settings.** Your preferences (auto-fallback on/off, history on/off) are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

**Clipboard.** When a link is successfully shortened, Shawty URL copies the shortened link to your device's clipboard so you can paste or share it. Clipboard contents can be read by other apps on your device — this is a general Android platform behavior, not something specific to or controlled by Shawty URL.

## What the app does not do

- No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs
- No advertising or ad SDKs
- No user accounts, sign-in, or device identifiers collected
- No data sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes
- No data collected from you beyond the URL you explicitly choose to shorten

## Third-party links

The Settings screen includes optional "Support the Dev" donation buttons (Ko-fi, Buy Me a Coffee, GitHub Sponsors) that open external websites in your browser if you tap them. Those sites are operated by third parties with their own privacy policies, unrelated to this app's data handling.

## Permissions

Shawty URL requests Android's `INTERNET` permission, used solely to contact the link-shortening service described above.

## Data retention and deletion

All data the app stores (history, settings) lives only on your device. Clearing history in-app, or uninstalling the app, permanently removes it — there is no copy retained anywhere else, since none is ever sent anywhere else.

## Children's privacy

Shawty URL is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, regardless of age.
